Navigating in Rooster

All of Rooster's navigation is done with the navbar.

Home: Tab

Rooster's home page is your landing screen after logging in. Here, you can see some of your closest/overdue reminders and the other pages and features easily.

Projects: Tab

The Projects tab lets see all your existing projects and create new ones. Clicking an existing project will let you edit its document and create, complete or delete reminders for it.

Reminders: Tab

Rooster’s Reminders tab lets you see all of your reminders in chronological order. Upcoming and completed reminders are separated for your convenience!

Dark Mode: Toggle

The Dark Mode toggle changes the color scheme of Rooster from light to dark. If you’re not enjoying dark mode, don’t worry! Clicking it again will switch it back.

Help: Tab

Right now, you’re in the help tab! Here, you can learn more about using Rooster. If you’re having problems with the website, contact the developers here.

Settings: Dropdown Menu

The settings dropdown lets you change some of your account’s information. This includes your name and profile picture, which you see on the home page! You can also logout with it.

More On Reminders

Reminders have their own unique buttons and icons!

Overdue Icon

Oh no- your reminder is overdue! This means that you've given your reminder a deadline, but you didn't mark it complete in time. These reminders will display at the top of all your lists to make sure you get to them quickly.

Complete Button

Marking a reminder complete means your task has been finished! Rooster will move it to the bottom of your reminder list to make sure your upcoming reminders are at the top. Be careful though, once marked complete, you can't go back!

Trash Button

Deleting a reminder removes it from your lists and projects permanenetly! Once deleted, there is no way for a user to view the reminder.
